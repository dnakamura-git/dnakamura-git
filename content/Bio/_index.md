Daisuke Nakamura is a master's student in Communicaiton Science at the University of Vienna, Austria. 

His interests include political communication, polarisation, radicalisation, and computational methods. He is especially interested in short videos on YouTube and TikTok, influencer content on these platforms, how this content shapes ordinary people's perceptions and emotions about in-group and out-group, and how we can analyze and measure these contents and reactions using computational methods.

He earned his BA in law and politics from Kyoto University, Japan in 2024. In 2022, he went on an exchange programme to the University of Melbourne, Australia, studying liberal arts and sciences.
